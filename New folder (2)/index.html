    Meeting Room Booking System
    by raiyanabdulbaten

    Meeting Room this function allows you to book any room on the display of a meeting room device. Users can see all the meeting rooms in the company directly on the reservation device, and choose and book an available room suitable for their needs quickly and easily, using a 'Projctor or laptop panel'. The scheduled meeting will be visible on the display of the meeting room device immediately after the reservation is made. On the center on the agenda, you can see the name, date and time of the meeting.

    You can also creat a account. Then you can sign in there and booking a meeting room of you'r choice.

    Basic Features

  • The system supports customers booking and able to modify them.
  • Customers can search room based on time
  • Customers able to cancel their booking from their account.
  • Staffs able to edit customers booking information.
  • The system sends booking confirmation email.
  • Customers able to check their booking status.

    Database:

    A database management system (DBMS) is system software for creating and managing databases. A DBMS makes it possible for end users to create, read, update and delete data in a database. The DBMS essentially serves as an interface between the database and end users or application programs, ensuring that data is consistently organized and remains easily accessible.
